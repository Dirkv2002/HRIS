What Each Stage Means
Application Received
Your application has been successfully received by our Human Capital team.
Reviewing
Your CV has passed the baseline criteria for the job and the HC team is currently reviewing your profile.
1st Interview
You have been chosen for a first round of online interviews. Please ensure that you book a timeslot for your interview in the Bookings tab.
Assessment
You have been selected to display your competency and skills in a work-based simulated assessment. Make sure to complete the allocated assessment in the Assessments tab.
2nd Interview
You have been selected as one of the final candidates for this position. The HC team will contact you soon regarding your in-person interview.
Final Assessment
You are among our top candidates. A final assessment will be conducted before a decision is made.
Offer / Outcome
The recruitment process is complete. You will be contacted by the HC team with the final outcome of your application.
