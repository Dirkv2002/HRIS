What Each Stage Means
Application Received
Your application has been successfully received by our Human Capital team.
Reviewing
Your CV has passed the baseline criteria and the HC team is currently reviewing your profile.
1st Interview
You have been chosen for a first round of online interviews. Please book a timeslot in the Bookings tab.
Assessment
You have been selected for a work-based simulated assessment. Complete it in the Assessments tab.
2nd Interview
You have been selected as one of the final candidates. The HC team will contact you regarding your in-person interview.
Final Assessment
You are among the top candidates. A final assessment will be conducted before the offer decision.
Offer / Outcome
The recruitment process is complete. The HC team will be in contact with the final outcome.
